Activate Your Child’s Account

Curiosity Machine users can post pictures and videos of their projects and interact with mentors in the Curiosity Machine community. To activate an account for a user under 13 years old, a parent or guardian must send us a signed consent form. This is in accordance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Download Parent Consent Form

To activate the account of your child under 13 years old, please send us a completed Parent Consent Form:

Via E-mail
Send to curiosity@iridescentlearning.org You can print the document, sign, scan or photograph, and email it to us or complete the consent form electronically using Adobe Acrobat Reader.
Via Fax
Fax to (888) 519-0108 with the title “COPPA Parent Consent”
Via Mail
Iridescent, 532 W. 22nd Street, Los Angeles, CA 90007

When we receive your Parent Consent Form, we will unlock your child’s account.

In the meantime, you can check out our design challenges and decide what to build first!
