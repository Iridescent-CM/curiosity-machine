Inspire the next generation of Inventors.

Become an online mentor with Curiosity Machine.

On Curiosity Machine, mentors support students as they build solutions to engineering design challenges. Mentors give feedback on individual projects and help students work through the engineering design process.

As a Curiosity Machine mentor, you will...

  • Improve your communication and mentoring skills
  • Inspire students by sharing the skills you use every day
  • Join our community of STEM professionals

Time Commitment

  • 90 minute online mentor training
  • 30 min / each project you provide feedback

Mentor Training

Before you begin mentoring, you’ll complete Curiosity Machine’s online mentor training.

In training, you’ll learn:

  • How to provide informative feedback to students
  • How to teach the Engineering Design Process and why it’s valuable
