Build something great together.

Connect to your child’s account and learn how to build together.

Use Curiosity Machine to make building (engineering?) a regular family activity. Work alongside your child to build solutions to engineering design challenges together.

We believe that creativity is like any other skill — anyone can develop it, it just takes practice! Use Curiosity Machine to practice by building hands-on engineering design challenges with your child. Team up to learn how things work and why they work as you build.

It’s free to sign up for Curiosity Machine, and people all over are using it to build interesting things — and to connect with other families!

  • Organize a Curiosity Machine playdate with friends and build a challenge in an afternoon
  • Form a Curiosity Machine Club and build together regularly, or build longer challenges over multiple weeks.
  • Bring Curiosity Machine to your child’s school.

Get Started

Sign up for a parent account to follow along as your child plans, builds, and tests their designs.

With a parent account you can:

  • See every step of every design your child builds, and watch your child’s portfolio of completed projects grow.
  • See all interactions your child has with their mentor(s).
  • Access parent trainings online

It is free to sign up for a parent account on Curiosity Machine. If your school has signed up for a membership, we will also offer expanded parent trainings to interested parents. Learn more.
