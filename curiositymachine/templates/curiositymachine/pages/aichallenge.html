Why AI?

The World Economic Forum estimates that 65 percent of children entering primary school today will work in completely new jobs that donâ€™t exist yet. With this new initiative, Iridescent aims to demystify Artificial Intelligence (AI) and AI tools for students and families and provide early introductions to the technology that is reshaping the workforce and encourage them to continue to learn about and engage with AI.

The Curiosity Machine AI Family Challenge invites families to learn about Artificial Intelligence technology. Over the next 2 years, 3rd-8th grade students and their families will explore core concepts of AI research, apply AI tools to solve problems in their communities and have the opportunity to enter their ideas into a global competition.

How does it work?

Stage 1:
Families will learn the foundations of Artificial Intelligence, build hands-on projects, and submit their work online through the Curiosity Machine platform.
Stage 2:
Families at in-person sites and individuals building online will identify problems in their local communities that AI tools could help solve and begin building solutions to those problems.
Stage 3:
We will host a series of regional celebration events for families to share their projects. The strongest projects will move on to the global competition, where families will have the opportunity to win scholarships and prizes.

Why should I participate?

  • Access to a curriculum with background information on AI, machine learning, data science, software, documentation and projects that have been created for social good
  • Have fun learning and competing in challenges that offer scholarships to winners and finalists
  • Ability to attend regional launch events, family programs, and regional/global competition events
  • Exposure to a mentor community that will support families participating in the competition
