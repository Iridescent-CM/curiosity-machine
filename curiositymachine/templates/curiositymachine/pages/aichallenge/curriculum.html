Curriculum

3 Stages of Learning

Stage 1: Foundations of AI

We explore some AI concepts through hands-on design challenges and build a community of family learning. Together, we learn:

  • AI is a computer that can be taught to learn things
  • Both artificial neural networks and parallel processing are important ways computers process data and make it possible for AI to learn new things
  • Self driving cars, robots and healthcare are some applications of AI

Stage 2: Exploring Robots

We explore robots through more design challenges and stay in touch with our community online. Together, we learn:

  • Simple mechanisms can make things move in a lot of different ways
  • Machines that can move give AI a way to interact with its surroundings

Stage 3: AI for the Community

We create a solution for a problem in our communities with AI tools and our family community continues to be a special place. Together we learn:

  • Machine learning is a type of AI, machine learning tools can be trained to learn new things when we input large amounts of data
  • It’s possible to train AI to recognize specific images, faces, or text
  • Skills to communicate and talk about AI
  • We can use AI to help solve some problems in our community

Final Capstone Project

  • Stage 3 will guide you to develop your own idea on how to identify and solve a problem in your community using AI technology
  • You’ll create a video explaining your idea and how you could incorporate the technology
