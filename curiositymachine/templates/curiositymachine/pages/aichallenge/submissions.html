Submissions

Enter the AI Family Challenge!

The submission window for the AI Family Challenge will open in December 2018 and will remain open until February 1st, 2019.

Please keep in mind that each family can only submit once and they will be placed in a division depending on the age of the oldest child registered for the challenge. All materials submitted online must be in English and judging will only happen online.

Participating families must submit:

  1. Family Team profile with names of all members
  2. At least 3 design challenges, from the AIFC challenges, on CuriosityMachine.org. Need help submitting design challenges? Check here
  3. 3-5 minute video that explains your project
  4. A title and a description of your project
  5. Family Post Survey
  6. (Optional) Up to 4 photos

Come back for more details as we approach the submission window!

If you have any questions, contact us at curiosity@iridescentlearning.org.
