Families

Do you want to learn about AI as a family? You can create a free family account and complete our program from home!

The basics:

  • You only need one account for your family to complete the 18 AI lessons

  • 6 family teams (3 from each division) will win a trip to the AI World Championship in California, USA. Check below for eligibility requirements.

Can my family submit to the AI World Championship?

Remember, any family can complete the program! To be eligible for the AI World Championship competition, your family must submit to either the Junior or Senior division:

Junior Division:

As of January 1, 2019, the oldest child in family is 11 or younger.

Senior Division:

As of January 1, 2019, the oldest child in family is 12-16.

  • Minimum 1 parent/guardian and 1 child per family team
  • 6 people maximum per family team
  • 2 adults per team maximum
