Coaches

Interested in Coaching?

AI Family Challenge coaches lead families through our specialized curriculum. Coaches who are not affiliated with a partner site may still help families in whatever context they normally work in:

  • For example, a coach who is an educator may facilitate an after school group with students and their families or encourage students to build with their families at home.
  • Or, coaches who work in their local library or other community-based organization can set up community programs to support family-based STEM learning of our AI design challenges.

Coaches always have access to all of our free online curriculum. If you a coaches who wants to sign up families for this challenge and be designated as their coach, complete this form. You will receive an email from us with a unique sign-up link that your families can use to be connected to your account.

The basics:

  • Lead students or families to complete the AI Family Challenge
  • Access AI Family Challenge content and resources to support your participants to complete the program
  • While we can not guarantee funding at this time, fill out this form in addition to creating a coach account if you would like to be considered for future opportunities
