FAQs

Which membership is right for me?

We are happy to meet to discuss school or multi-site memberships and add ons. We are unable to meet to discuss a classroom membership

Can I pay by purchase order?

We will work with you on a purchase order for school and multi-site memberships. We are unable to accommodate purchase orders for classroom memberships.

My school doesn't have the budget, how can I use Curiosity Machine?

Feel free to sign up and complete our free design challenges.

How do I connect all student, parent or educator accounts to my membership?

Follow the instructions to complete a membership account form. We can create the accounts for your membership based on the information you send us.
