We're evolving

As of August 2, Iridescent, parent organization to the AI Family Challenge and Technovation programs, will change its name to Technovation. We’re excited to continue to empower youth and adults to be leaders, creators and problem-solvers through technology.

We’ve also renamed our programs to clearly connect them to Technovation and address feedback we’ve received from our partners. The educational focus of each program is not changing.

AI Family Challenge will be called Technovation Families.

The program brings together families, schools, communities and industry mentors to learn, play, and create with artificial intelligence.

Season 2 of our AI challenge is now open. Sign-up today.

STEM challenges created for Curiosity Machine will remain available on this website. See all the challenges.

Technovation will be called Technovation Girls.

The program equips young women ages 10-18 with the skills to become tech entrepreneurs and leaders.

Learn more about Technovation.

We look forward to this next step in our growth and to continuing to collaborate with our partners to empower girls and families to be real-world problem solvers using technology.
