Choose Your Role

Families

Any family can complete the program! Family teams share 1 Technovation Families account, and must have:

  • Minimum 1 parent/guardian and 1 child ages 16 or younger per family team
  • 6 people maximum per family team
  • 2 adults per team maximum
Families will participate in either the Senior or Junior division, determined by the oldest child's birth date.

Sign up for Technovation Families

Program Leaders

Program leaders coordinate Technovation Families in their local communities. Because every community is different, programs can range in scale from small school clubs to multi-site programs organized in partnership with a local community organization or school district.

What all Program Leaders have in common is a passion for helping families in their community to learn the skills they need to succeed in the future.

Volunteers

Volunteer as a mentor and connect with families as they learn about technology and create tech products to solve community problems.

Mentor time commitment ranges from 2-50 hours, with In-person and remote opportunities available. Training is provided.

Additional volunteer opportunities as volunteer judges, guest speakers, and content partners are available as well!
