Activate Your Child’s Account

Curiosity Machine users can post pictures and videos of their projects and interact with mentors in the Curiosity Machine community. To activate an account for a user under 13 years old, a parent or guardian must complete the consent form which was sent to their email. This is in accordance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

To activate the account of your child under 13 years old, please complete the online Parent Consent Form which was sent to your email. Please allow up to 30 minutes for the activation of your child's account after you fill out the form.

When you completed the Parent Consent Form in your email inbox, we will unlock your child’s account within 30 minutes. Please email curiosity@iridescentlearning.org if your account in not activated within 30 minutes of signing, or an email was not recived.

In the meantime, you can check out our design challenges and decide what to build first!
