Activate Your Child’s Account

Curiosity Machine users can post pictures and videos of their projects and interact with mentors in the Curiosity Machine community. To activate an account for a user under 13 years old, a parent or guardian must complete the consent form which was sent to their email. This is in accordance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Next Steps

  1. You should be receiving an email to activate your underage account
  2. A parent or guardian must follow the link on the email, fill in required fields and sign
  3. This account will be activated up to 30 minutes after you have subitted the form

Email curiosity@iridescentlearning.org if you do not receive the email or if your account in not activated after 30 minutes of signing.

In the meantime, you can check out our design challenges and decide what to build first!
