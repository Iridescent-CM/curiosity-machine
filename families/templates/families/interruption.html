To activate your account...

Parents and guardians, please follow these directions to register for the program:

  1. Follow this link to complete the AI Challenge Pre-survey.
  2. Check your email and follow directions to sign your consent form. Once you sign, you will receive a copy via email of the form.
  3. Check your email and follow directions to verify your email account.
  4. Once you complete all three items, you will receive an email that your account is activated. This can take up to 20 minutes. Try refreshing or re-logging in if you are unable to access the design challenges!

Email curiosity@iridescentlearning.org if you have completed the three steps and your account is not activated within 30 minutes.
