I. PRIVACY NOTICE REGARDING COLLECTION OF INFORMATION FROM CHILDREN UNDER 13

Curiosity Machine limits the collection of personally identifiable information from children to only that which is necessary to obtain parental consent or to participate in a Curiosity Machine program or activity after we have obtained parental consent. Curiosity Machine is prohibited from conditioning a child's participation in an activity at Curiosity Machine on the disclosure of more personally identifiable information than is reasonably necessary to participate in such activity. Children under 13 years old are not allowed to subscribe or create an account without a parent or guardian's consent.

Parental Consent

We require a parent or guardian to consent before a child under the age of 13 can use any Curiosity Machine products or services or participate in any Curiosity Machine programs or activities.

Parents can provide consent in person or by fax or postal mail for their children to provide personally identifiable information to us. The parental consent enables children under 13 to access Curiositymachine.org products or services or participate in Curiosity Machine programs or activities. Upon receipt of the parental consent form, Curiosity Machine will send you an e-mail with a user name and password for your child. Upon receipt of the user name and password, please reply to the e-mail, acknowledging receipt of the user name and password for your child to use on curiositymachine.org.

The consent form must be signed and emailed to curiosity@iridescentlearning.org (you can use electronic signature). If not available, please provide consent by fax or postal mail.

How You Can Control the Information We Collect About You ?

You can always choose not to submit your personally identifiable information to Curiosity Machine, but if you do not submit personal information, you may not be able to access many of the features, products, programs, and services that we offer.

If you do not wish to receive e-mail messages from Curiositymachine.org about features, products, programs, or services that may be of interest, you can unsubscribe at any time. We will include an opt-out web link or reply e-mail address in each e-mail message. After unsubscribing, you will be removed from our e-mail database. Please allow up to 10 business days to process your request. After unsubscribing, you may still receive e-mail messages about the status of your account, to resolve technical issues, or to confirm a new subscription.

If you wish to make changes to any personally identifiable information you have provided to us, or if you have any questions about what we do with your personally identifiable information, please contact us by sending an e-mail to Curiosity@IridescentLearning.org.
