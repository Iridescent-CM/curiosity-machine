Build your own Curiosity Machine community!

Curiosity Machine is a resource you can use to inspire students to become inventors while they build Engineering Design Challenges. Anyone with a passion for learning and exploration can build a Curiosity Machine community--no science background is necessary!


Curiosity Machine features:

  • Free access to science and engineering content and Engineering Design Challenges that anyone can facilitate with simple materials
