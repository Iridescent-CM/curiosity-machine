Parent accounts are closing

In order to better meet the needs of our users, we’re closing parent accounts, but all parents will have the option to convert their accounts to our new family accounts, student, educator or coach accounts. This change doesn’t mean that there is no longer a place for parents – you are vital to Curiosity Machine, and we are confident that you’ll find a better fit in one of our upgraded account options.

What can I do?

Choose another account type

If you want to keep using Curiosity Machine, you can use the button above to pick another type of account. Otherwise no action is needed, and your existing account will be made inactive on June 1, 2018.

Type of accounts:

  • Family account:
    • Access all of our design challenges and resources (when available) for free
    • You and your child/children share an account
    • Focus on our AI Family Challenge design challenges
    • Have access to additional resources
    • Have the opportunity to submit to the official competition
  • Coach account:
    • Access all of our design challenges and resources for free
    • Access additional resources related to our AI Family Challenge
  • Student account:
    • Access all of our design challenges for free
    • Upload your progress to the site
  • Educator account:
    • Access all of our design challenges and resources (when available) for free

If you have any concerns please contact us at curiosity@iridescentlearning.org!
