How to use these units

The following units were developed to inspire students to be curious, creative, and persistent learners. Lessons are project-based, providing a rich and engaging learning experience. Each lesson is also aligned to Next Generation Science Standards in Engineering Design, grades 6-8.

Each unit is supported by:

  • A Lesson Guide with 5 lesson plans.
  • Student Workbook with worksheets for each lesson.
  • An explanation of NGSS standards alignment for each lesson.
  • Individual mentorship for students on Curiosity Machine.
  • Individual student design portfolios on Curiosity Machine.
  • An Educator Portal to monitor student design progress and mentorship.
  • An online training for you to learn more.
