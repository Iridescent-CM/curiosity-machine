How to use these units

Each unit has a series of Curiosity Machine’s engineering design challenges scaffolded together to teach the engineering design process in a classroom setting. We have created supporting tools for each unit to help you bring this hands-on, project based learning to your students!

The basics about each unit:

  • Aligned to the Next Generation Science Standards, with a focus on 6-8th grade Middle School Engineering Design standards, but can be adapted for use into any classroom.
  • Contains an Educator Resource Book with five hands-on lesson plans, Frequently Asked Questions and appendix sections.
  • Contains a Student Workbook with worksheets that support each lesson.
  • Every engineering design challenge in the units is posted and supported on CuriosityMachine.org for you to reference at anytime.

Curiosity Machine provides you with free access to engineering design challenges developed by scientists and engineers and tested by educators.

  • Each challenge guides a student through the steps of the engineering design process, from planning to reflection.
  • By documenting their building process online, students get the chance to iterate on their inventions with the support of a trained scientist or engineer mentor. This support is a unique structure for providing highly individualized feedback to each student.
