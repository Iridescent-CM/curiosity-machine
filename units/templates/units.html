Engineering Design Units
Inspire your students to be curious, creative, and persistent learners!

Curiosity Machine units are project-based and provide a hands-on, engaging learning experience for your students.

Each unit is supported by:

  • An Educator Lesson Guide with 5 lesson plans and Frequently Asked Questions section.
  • A Student Workbook with worksheets that support each lesson.
  • Next Generation Science Standards alignment for students in grades 6-8.
  • Individual mentorship for students who use Curiosity Machine.
  • Individual student design portfolios on Curiosity Machine.

Learn more about Curiosity Machine here, or send inquiries about training for educators to use these resources to curiosity@iridescentlearning.org
