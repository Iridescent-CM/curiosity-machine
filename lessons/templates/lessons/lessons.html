Communities using technology for change

Our free, hands-on artificial intelligence (Al) education challenge brings families, schools, communities, and technology professionals together to learn, play and create solutions to real-world problems with Al.

Learn what Al is, how it works, and how to use it to solve a problem you care about through our 10-lesson challenge. At the conclusion of the challenge you will have completed a project together: An Al invention that solves a problem you care about in your community. Submit your project to our global competition for a chance to attend the annual Technovation World Summit in the U.S. as a finalist and present your idea to a panel of judges at the Championship Event in August.
